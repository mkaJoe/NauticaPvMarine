About LUXURY Yacht PV

We offer private charters and luxury yacht sales. Our luxury yachts are extremely spacious. Featuring a full bar, galley, dining area, lounging areas for relaxing, all with air conditioning to keep you and your guests cool. You can also enjoy your favorite playlist with our HD surround sound music system.

Our fishing charters are equipped with top tier equipment for sport fishing lovers. We also have snorkel gear for all guests to use at their leisure, our experts will always provide you with top of the line service, great food, premium spirits and take you to spectacular destinations.

Our top of the line ships

Choose from a wide selection of boats, from luxury yachts to classic sailboats, we have a variety of boats available to suit your needs. If you have a special request, we have a specialized team that will help you choose the boat that meets your wishes.
